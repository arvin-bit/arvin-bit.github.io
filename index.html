Team X

Now what is Team X? Well, i'm here to tell you. In Team X: Covert Ops players take on the role of an elite operative for a top-secret special operations team known as "Team X", operating in Iran. Embark on dangerous and covert mission across a variety of locations within Iran, relying on stealth, strategy, and tactical skills to complete objectives and eliminate threats. With access to a variety of weapons and gadgets, players must use their wits and skills to infiltrate, survive, and succeed in this thrilling and intense action-packed game.
